Our Story

Vellore's Most
Advanced Fitness Centre

Hundreds Fitness is the most palatial and advanced fitness centre in Vellore — with branches in Gandhi Nagar and VIT Road. We are a proud part of the Hundreds Group, built to bring world-class training to every member.

Our philosophy is simple: every workout should be structured, motivating, and personalised to your goals. Whether you're just starting out or training at an elite level, our certified trainers are with you every step of the way.

Great ambience, professional staff, premium equipment — this is fitness without compromise.
